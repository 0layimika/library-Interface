SIYU Library

SIYU library is a library management system used for managing your library.

We handle managing your books as well as tracking your books to the borrower.

Siyu library is aimed at helping students know the available books in the library and to also help the librarian manage and track books in the library.
Books available in the library range from educational books for all colleges and departments to journals, newspapers, historical books and even storybooks for reading in leisure time.
